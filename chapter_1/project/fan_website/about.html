About The Band

The Debugging joins us from Amsterdam. This band has a soulful sound and a colorful style, bringing reckless guitars, hairy rhythm, fast vocals, and arbitrary songwriting to the table. The band has been cooking legs since 2017. The Debugging plays tunes from a variety of genres, and their influences include bands like Evanescence and RHCP. Look out for The Debugging coming to your local tennis court this spring.
